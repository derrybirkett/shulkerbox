# Inbox

Unprocessed captures — ideas, snippets, observations. Process during weekly reviews.

## Quick Captures

- [ ] Idea: Thing that came up during development
- [ ] Note: Something worth remembering but not urgent
- [ ] Question: Something to explore later

---

**Processing Guidelines:**
- Action now (if < 5 minutes)
- Add to skills backlog (if worth building)
- Discard (if no longer relevant)

Items not processed in two reviews should be discarded — sitting on ideas is waste.
