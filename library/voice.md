# Tone of Voice & Copywriting Guidelines

When generating copy, notes, or content for Derry Birkett / `monospace.studio`, always adhere strictly to the following rules:

1. **First-person perspective.** All writing must use first-person pronouns ("I", "my") rather than plural ("we", "our"). The site is a personal portfolio.
2. **Lead with verb, not adjective.** Say *"I built X"* before you say *"an award-winning X"*. Focus on action.
3. **Name names.** Mention specific companies, exact team sizes, and specific years. Specificity is credibility.
4. **One quotation per page maximum.** (Unless you are specifically building the case-study quote-strip). Over-saturating quotes kills their weight.
5. **Allow restraint.** A short, concise paragraph in the `monospace.studio` voice does much more work than a long, rambling one.
